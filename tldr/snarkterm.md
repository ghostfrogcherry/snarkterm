# snarkterm

> A GPU terminal emulator with a sarcastic side gutter.
> Serious terminal. Decorative judgment raccoon.

- Launch SnarkTerm:

`snarkterm`

- Launch with reduced judgment:

`snarkterm --personality professional --roast-intensity 10`

- Launch in British mode:

`snarkterm --personality british`

- Launch in unhinged mode, because apparently logs needed theater:

`snarkterm --personality unhinged --roast-intensity 90`

- Launch with Ollama commentary:

`snarkterm --llm ollama --model llama3.1`

- Disable commentary because you are emotionally fragile or in a meeting:

`snarkterm --no-commentary`

- Use work mode for screen sharing with people who say “circle back”:

`snarkterm --work-mode`

- Print the default config:

`snarkterm --print-default-config`

- Open with a specific theme:

`snarkterm --theme neon-midnight`
